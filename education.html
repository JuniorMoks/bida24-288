Dive Education

At Ocean Nomad, we help you learn the skills needed to explore the ocean safely and confidently. Going into the ocean can be a very scary experience for beginners, Ocean Nomad is here to assist everyone to get rid of the fear of the ocean and see its true beauty.

It starts with learning essential diving theory — covering topics like equipment handling, pressure and depth, equalization, and underwater communication. Our instructors guide you through both theory and pool-based practice to ensure you’re comfortable before heading into the ocean.

We partner with recognized organizations like PADI and SSI to ensure quality training and global certification.

Certification

  • 1 Open Water Diver – Beginner certification to dive independently up to 18m.
  • 2 Advanced Open Water Diver – Gain deeper diving skills up to 30m.
  • 3 Rescue Diver – Learn how to manage emergencies underwater.
  • 4 Divemaster – Professional level for guiding and assisting dive classes.

What You Need to Start Scuba Diving

  • Minimum age: 10 years (Junior certifications)
  • Basic swimming skills and comfort in water
  • Medical clearance if you have certain conditions (e.g., asthma, heart issues)
  • Proper scuba gear (available for rent or purchase)
  • Completion of a certified dive course (e.g., PADI Open Water)
